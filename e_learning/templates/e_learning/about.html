ABOUT VIRTUAL-CLASS

Virtual-Class is an online learning platform with an e-library as one of the key functionalities. The platform enables learners to access learning materials and interact with their teachers in a way similar to a traditional classroom. It also enables a teacher to chat with colleagues in the similar subject areas.

Additionally, the system has an e-library that is accessible to all users; enabling a book-by-book access of all books in a typical secondary school library. Teachers also have an opportunity to recommend books for their learners.

Our Motto:

Learn anything, anytime

OTHER FEATURES

E-Library

The system has an e-library where learners and teachers can access soft copies of most books in their school libraries at any time. With this service, learners from schools that have no library can access educational content just like those students from schools with very good libraries.

E-Book

This is a special feature on that enables teachers to recommend and upload specific books to their learners for reference or additional revision.

The virtual class team comprises a talent pool of professionals from the fields of Education and Information Technology. The team is broadly divided into three categories: A quality assurance/policy expert; Subject Heads of Departments and the IT team.

The quality assurance/policy expert is responsible for providing the overall framework for the operation of the system and ensuring everything posted on the platform is in compliance with the policies of the government and the ministry of Education and Sports.

The Subject department heads are responsible for ensuring the quality of the content uploaded by the different subject teachers. The IT is responsible for the overall management; update and security of data of the users.

Virtual Class platform is owned by Agriculture Cluster Development Limited; a private company incorporated in Uganda. However, the platform operates as a semi-autonomous entity with a separate management team.

For more information on how the system works, click on the Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs).
