WHAT IS VIRTUAL-CLASS

Virtual-Class is an online learning platform with an e-library as one of the key functionalities.The e-library enables all users to access books that can aid thier learning. The platform enables learners to access learning materials and interact with their teachers in a way similar to a traditional classroom. It also enables teachers to interact among themselves.

Additionally, the system has an e-library that is accessible to all users; enabling a book-by-book access of all books in a typical secondary school library. Teachers also have an opportunity to recommend books for their learners.

MISSION

To be the leading platform that supports, encourages and enables students to embrace e-learning and build the capacity of teachers and trainers in using digital technologies for enhancing teaching.

MOTTO

Learn anything, anytime

VISION

A generation that embraces Information Technology as a fulcrum of academic and professional excellence.

OTHER FEATURES

E-Book is a special feature on that enables teachers to recommend and upload specific books to their learners for reference or additional revision.

Group Discussion this system feature enables students share ideas with their colleagues regarding particular subject topics.

ChatRoom is a system feature that enables teachers to interact among themselves.

The virtual class team comprises a talent pool of professionals from the fields of Education and Information Technology. The team is broadly divided into three categories: A quality assurance/policy expert; Subject Heads of Departments and the IT team.

  • The quality assurance/policy expert is responsible for providing the overall framework for the operation of the system and ensuring everything posted on the platform is in compliance with the policies of the government and the ministry of Education and Sports.

  • The Subject departmental heads are responsible for ensuring the quality of the content uploaded by the different subject teachers meets set standards.

  • The IT team is responsible for the overall management; update and security of data of the users.
