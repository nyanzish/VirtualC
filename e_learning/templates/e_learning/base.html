 VIRTUAL-CLASS

Virtual-Class is an online learning platform with an e-library as one of the key functionalities. The platform enables learners to access learning materials and interact with their teachers in a way similar to a traditional classroom. It also enables a teacher to chat with colleagues in the similar subject areas.
