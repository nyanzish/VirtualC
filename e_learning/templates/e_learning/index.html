Welcome to Virtual-Class e-learning platform
“Learn anything, anytime”

Virtual-Class is an online learning platform that enables students study online, access learning materials and interact with teachers in a way similar to a traditional classroom with lessons conducted inform of videos and notes in pdf files.

With E-library as one of the system's key functionalities, it enables students access books recommended by teachers in different subjects. Our current focus is on secondary level (Both Ordinary and Advanced levels). However, an expansion of the site is underway to include college (tertiary level).
