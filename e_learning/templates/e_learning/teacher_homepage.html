Welcome to Your Dashboard

Welcome to Virtual Class Teacher Admin Side, here you will be able;

  • To upload notes for the subjects you applied to teach. You can also edit or delete incase of changes,
  • To share or recommend books through E-Books for the learners
  • You will be able to share ideas with different teachers through the Chat room.

Note:
Before uploading notes you will be required to provide an overview of the subject which includes what you plan to teach the learners and what skills the students will possibly gain from your lessons.
Subject introductory video will help the learners to grasp the key conceptsof the topic. However you can skip it incase you donot have a video yet.
Once you are able to create a video, you can proceed to upload.

How to upload Subject Overview

  • On the side navbar provided, click the subject you want to upload an overview for e.g Mathematics,Physics,English etc.
  • After choosing the subject, click the class you want to upload an overview for.
  • Fill in the overview form and finally upload your overview content.
  • Subject Image: It helps learners to identify the teacher. Teachers are encouraged to upload images either of themselves or any image that represents the topic or subject. The images uploaded should be preferably of high quality.
  • Pricing: Here the teacher is free to set a price for his content. However, teachers will be given guidance on how this pricing is determined.

How to upload Notes or Content

  • On the side navbar provided, re-click the subject you want to upload notes for.
  • After choosing the subject, click the class you want to upload content for.
  • Upload your content by filling the upload form that will be provided.
    Click Upload Content Button and you will be Done!
